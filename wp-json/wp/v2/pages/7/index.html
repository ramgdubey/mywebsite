TAGORE COLLEGE was established in the year 1982 by the Ministry of Rehabilitation, in the memory of Late Rabindranath Tagore-poet,writer,composer,philosopher,painter & social reformer. The college made a humble beginning with just 72 students and courses like Prep(arts), Prep(science) and Pre-Medical. The college now has over 3000 students in different courses and is one of the India’s top college.<\/strong><\/p>\n

The College has one of the largest libraries in terms of space and collection. It provides library services with the introduction of new and modern ICT infrastructure. The library provides access to electronic resources to its users from Delhi University Library System(DULS) & UGC-INFONET Consortium.<\/p>\n
